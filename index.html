We've got what you need!

GLADIUS is a smart underwater drone that shoots 4K videos, optimized by light sensors and algrithms. Built for filming, observing and exploring, the Gladius is a portable and user-friendly underwater drone that is able to dive up to 100 meters deep with a horizontal range of 500 meters.

Key Features

Ultra HD Camera

On-board 1080P or 4K camera. Capture high quality photo and video directly to internal storage or live stream your dive in real-time.

Semi-Tether System

Wifi-buoy and Semi-tether connection increases range to 500m and 100m deep, triple the industry average.​

Video Game-like

Piloting is easy with its quad thrusters, four degrees of freedom and gaming-like controller.​

3.5 Hours Baattery Life

Onboard LIPO smart batteries on the either side, providing a run time of over 3 hours.​

Video Game-like Simple Controls

Simply insert your iOS or Android smartphone into the remote control. With similarities to a gaming system, you move Gladius through the operation of two joysticks. One operates the forward, back, and turning of the drone (think of it like walking), while the other operates the direction in which the camera faces (think of it like moving your head). Your smartphone rests in the center of the remote controller, so you can view the live stream of your underwater exploration.
